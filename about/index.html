About Moi :)

You ever just…want a digital archive of your brain?

I am a passionate learner, likely to be found working on a complex problem, punching a bag at the gym or creating some music.

Currently studying at The University of Sydney, my interests range from understanding the power of the mind to particle physics. Most of my writeups are about projects I’m working on but you’ll find I occasionally digress into poetry, my personal learnings and opinions on intriguing matters.

This is why my blog is called No Agenda. With so many interesting things in the world to write about, build, contemplate and observe, I find it a little more than difficult to specify a niche for this space.

Then again, I’ve never liked constraining archetypes so let me use the words of Steve Jobs in hoping this page calls interest upon “the crazy ones, the misfits, the rebels, the troublemakers”.

Without further ado, let’s take a dive!
